FAQS


DO YOU PROVIDE HANDS ON TRAINING?

Yes! Every implementation comes with hands on training for all your
staff and product managers, pre-sales training is also available, if
required, to help encourage your sales staff.

CAN I BECOME A SIP TRUNK or PEER TERMINATION SERVICE PROVIDER?

VProvider handles multiple carrier peers and every peer can be
configured to use its own independent pricing, you can even host a
3rd party SIP peer on their behalf.

HOW SECURE IS THE VPROVIDER SOFT SWITCH?

Security is standard with VProvider, we include VSecure, which is our
IP Tables IP Address blocking software, with every self-hosted
deployment. It is installed as a service on your Linux server.

WHAT IS THE COST OF VPROVIDER?

The cost of VProvider is dependent on the total number of
concurrent calls you require. Concurrent call tiers range from 30 to
over 5000 concurrent calls per cluster.

DOES VPROVIDER SUPPORT AUTOMATIC ENDPOINT PROVISIONING?

Yes, VProvider supports zero touch automatic provisioning of both
Yealink and Htek and automatic provisioning of many other
endpoint manufactures.

WHAT ARE THE CURRENT DEVELOPMENT CYCLES FOR VPROVIDER?

Development releases with new features and voice enhancements are
released roughly every 12 months. Custom developments are also
available.
